About Us

Opésh Overseas is a globally aspiring export house delivering clinically validated Ayurvedic and phytochemistry-based nutraceuticals, rooted in India’s scientific heritage and powered by cutting-edge innovations. In partnership with GPLife Healthcare Pvt. Ltd. (INR 500 Cr turnover), we are revolutionizing preventive and regenerative healthcare across the globe with scientifically-backed plant-based formulations.

Our formulations are 100% natural, AI-optimized, and excipient-free, developed through 19 years of R&D. We specialize in:

  • Kidney Disease (CKD)
  • Arthritis & Joint Pain
  • Heart & Gut Health
  • Anti-aging & Cell Regeneration

Global Vision
