Welcome to Opésh Overseas

Opésh Overseas is a next-generation global export house from India, built on the mission to deliver clinically validated plant-based supplements rooted in Ayurvedic science. With a strong focus on international quality standards and regulatory alignment, we aim to build trust through excellence in every aspect of our operations.

About Us

Founded with a vision to bring India's ancient wellness science to the global platform, Opésh Overseas is committed to delivering safe, effective, and science-backed dietary supplements. Our approach integrates traditional wisdom with cutting-edge phytochemistry research to offer transformative health solutions across continents.

Products & Science

Our product portfolio includes solutions for chronic conditions such as diabetes, arthritis, liver health, kidney health, and longevity. Each product is 100% plant-based and undergoes rigorous research, safety study, and quality assurance. All formulations are excipient-free, relying solely on synergistic phytoconstituents for efficacy.
